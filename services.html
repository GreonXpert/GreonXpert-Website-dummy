Zero Carbon

AI-powered SaaS for automated Scope 1–3 GHG accounting, decarbonization planning, and carbon-credit marketplace integration.

  • Automated Data Integration: Ingests data via API, CSV, or IoT sensors.
  • Comprehensive GHG Accounting: Real-time calculation for Scope 1, 2, & 3 emissions.
  • AI-Driven Decarbonization: Generates SBTi-aligned reduction roadmaps.
  • Integrated Marketplace: Lists, facilitates, and trades verified carbon credits.
  • Real-Time Dashboards: Custom KPIs and one-click stakeholder-ready reports.

ESG Link

AI-powered SaaS platform streamlining ESG management—automating data collection, materiality analysis, and reporting across global standards.

  • Multi-Standard Reporting: Supports BRSR, GRI, SASB, TCFD, CSRD, and custom frameworks.

  • Security & Scalability: SOC 2-compliant, end-to-end encryption, and GDPR-ready.
