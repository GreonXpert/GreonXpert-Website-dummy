Our IoT Products

Rugged, secure, and intelligent hardware for seamless environmental data collection.

Greon Connect

A plug-and-play communication controller that captures and securely streams energy and emissions data from on-site sensors to sustainability platforms.

  • Flexible Protocols: Supports Modbus RTU/TCP, MQTT, HTTP, LoRaWAN, and more.
  • Seamless Integration: Streams standardized JSON/TLS data to Zero Carbon and ESG Link.
  • Secure & Manageable: Features end-to-end encryption and secure over-the-air (OTA) firmware updates.

Greon Meter

A high-accuracy metering solution for electrical energy measurement, featuring dual-protocol outputs and on-device analytics.

  • High-Precision: Measures single and three-phase electricity with ±0.2% accuracy.
  • Edge Analytics: Computes consumption, peak demand, and power factor on-device.
  • Secure & Scalable: TLS-encrypted streams, bulk provisioning, and remote diagnostics.

Greon Track

A plug-and-play telematics module that reads vehicle data via OBD-II/CAN bus to deliver actionable insights into fleet efficiency and emissions.

  • OBD-II & CAN-Bus: Reads standard PIDs and heavy-vehicle J1939 data.
  • Real-time Analytics: Calculates fuel consumption, CO₂e emissions, and idle time on-device.
  • Advanced Security: Features secure boot, hardware root-of-trust, and encrypted data streams.
