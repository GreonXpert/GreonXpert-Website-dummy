About GroenXpert

We are a forward-thinking environmental solutions company dedicated to enabling sustainable transformation across industries.

Our Story

GroenXpert Pvt Ltd was established with the vision of accelerating climate action. We specialize in carbon accounting, ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) services, and GHG emissions management for businesses of all scales by leveraging innovative digital tools, expert consulting, and data-driven strategies.

From early-stage ESG research for top corporates to developing our end-to-end ZeroCarbon platform, our journey is rooted in bridging ground-level realities with digital transformation.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with the digital infrastructure and expert guidance needed to measure, manage, and reduce their environmental impact.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in digital sustainability, driving climate-positive change through technology, innovation, and collaboration.

Our Expertise

End-to-End Carbon Management

Our ZeroCarbon Platform offers a complete solution from data collection (Manual, API, IoT) to emission calculation (Scope 1, 2, 3) and reporting, adhering to global standards like GHG Protocol and IPCC.

Comprehensive ESG Reporting

We assist in ESG reporting and sustainability disclosures by designing templates aligned with global frameworks, analyzing corporate reports, and mapping performance metrics.

Innovation & People-Driven Growth

Our culture emphasizes clean UI/UX, scalable architecture (React, Node.js, MongoDB), and seamless integration. We believe in continuous learning and agile development.

Driving climate-positive change through technology, innovation, and collaboration.
